Classified Profiles

We're offering Conductors, Adjudicators, Composers and Arrangers the ability to add more details to their page including a biography and contact information. The site is becoming ever more popular with 1000s of visitors and over 100,000 page views per month. Our visitors are even using the site to find and research potential conductors for their bands.

With your profile you can:

  • put a detailed biography on your page
  • link to your website
  • advertise your services for all to see
  • combine contest results, adjudications and compositions/arrangements on the same page
  • include your picture
  • include contact details
  • have no adverts on your page

See an example for David Read.

In order to build your profile:

  • sign in (or register for an account),
  • find your page (it doesn't matter which one if you have adjudications and conducting history),
  • click the Personalise This Page link.

You can update your profile at any point, simply by signing in and clicking Your User Profile on the menu.
