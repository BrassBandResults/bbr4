Advertising

This site is free to use, the associated costs involved in running the site are paid for through advertising and pro accounts. Some of this advertising is from Google Adsense (https://www.google.com/adsense) and will result in cookies being written to your machine, unless you change your browser settings to stop this happening. The site will work fine without these cookies, but we would appreciate you signing up for a pro account to cover lost revenue.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (http://www.google.com/analytics/) for tracking which pages are viewed on the site. You can opt out of google analytics with a browser plugin. This helps us find out which parts of the site are the most popular, so we can target our time at the things people like to see. Google Analytics writes four cookies to your machine, unless you change your browser settings to stop this. The site will work fine without these cookies, but we will not have as much information about the pages that users have looked at.

Logging In

If you login to an account on the site, we need to write a session cookie. If you remove this cookie, the site will not work properly.

Account Expiry

If you don't log into your account for a period of one year, we will expire the account. Any information entered is kept, but we will remove your email address from our records. This means that password reminder emails will no longer work, although you can (if you know your password) login to the account and enter an up to date email address to reactivate.
